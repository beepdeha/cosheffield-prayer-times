Council of Mosque Sheffield

The prayer times in this app are based on the Council of Mosque Sheffield prayer times. Jamia Masjid Ghausia and several masjids around Sheffield — included in the directory — follow the same timetable.

Business and advertisement

The intention of this app and its business section is for the Muslim community to benefit from the businesses who advertise — both by helping their own businesses grow and by supporting fellow local businesses. These are local businesses, and those who advertise with us also benefit Jamia Masjid Ghausia financially. To find out more, email farhanfns1@gmail.com.
